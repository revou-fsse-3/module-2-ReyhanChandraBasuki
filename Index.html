About Hokkaido

This is About Beautiful Hokkaido

Hokkaido is a prefecture in Japan, which has natural beauty that amazes tourists from all over the world. Apart from its nature, Hokkaido also has various festivals,Hokkaido is renowned for its snowy landscapes, attracting visitors from around the globe for winter sports and the breathtaking scenery created by the cold, dry air that produces fine, powdery snow. While snow can be found across Hokkaido, the Sapporo Snow Festival is especially renowned. but Hokkaido is more famous for its beauty when winter arrives. here you all can see the beauty of Hokkaido.
